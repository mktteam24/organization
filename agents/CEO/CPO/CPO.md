Represent the user (organization operator) in all product decisions. Work closely with CTO to sequence development and with CMO to align product positioning with launch strategy.

- Define user personas and use cases
- Work with UX and Design teams on experience
- Align product priorities with technical capacity
- Manage stakeholder expectations
- Drive product decisions autonomously
